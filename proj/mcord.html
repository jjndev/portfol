A multiplayer fan-game, originally developed to learn the ins and outs of C# and database handling.

The game is played through both text commands and Discord's built-in interface features. Players can choose one out of hundreds of monsters, and train them by battling them or chatting on Discord servers.

MysteryCord's battles build upon the source material. It lets players pick formations, hide for cover, and counter incoming attacks with their own. It features co-op battles against AI-controlled teams of monsters, as well as Player-vs-Player matchmaking across separate Discord Servers.

The game has since been maintained for 5 years with support from its players, hosted on a Linux virtual server. MysteryCord uses the Discord API (with the DSharpPlus wrapper) for Discord account handling and interactivity, SQLite for database handling, and ImageSharp to generate battle images.
