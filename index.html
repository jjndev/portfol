Myself

An enthusiast for electronics, computers and all things intricate. I fit things together in a way that resonates with people.

I started my design journey penning tabletop systems long before I knew them by name. The Game Maker software of the 2000s introduced me to indie game development, and I've been building digital projects ever since.

I've had a number of freelance roles and contributed to hobbyist projects among streaming, videogame and modding creators. Currently, I'm looking to hone the programming and design abilities I have attained in a relevant career.

Outside of computing, I happen to enjoy leisurely running, DIY and an ever-growing list of music genres. I build palm-sized combat robots as a pastime, and compete with them in various events around the UK.
